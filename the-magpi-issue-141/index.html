The MagPi Issue 141

26 Apr 2024

In the latest MagPi - Issue #141 - I have a chat with Oscar Vermeulen who has just launched his PiDP-10 kit, the latest in a series of Raspberry Pi-based DEC computer recreations. This is his most ambitious yet, with a two-thirds scale reproduction of the legendary system. It comes complete with MIT's ITS OS, which made the computer ideal for hacking - leading to the very first computer game - Spacewar.

Also featured is a tutorial on AI on Raspberry Pi, a case round-up and some cool stuff on robots. It's a packed issue.

Look out for the next issue where I'll be reviewing the PDP-10 kit.
